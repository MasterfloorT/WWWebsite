Who are we?

Wireless Waiter is founded by a team of three experienced IT professionals who decided to quit their day jobs to create Wireless Waiter, a remote ordering platform specifically designed to help theatres get back up and running after the impact of Covid-19.

Jenny Geall

Co-Founder & Technical Lead

(BA in Engineering, Cambridge University)

Jenny has over twenty years of experience in delivering national public and private sector IT systems both as an Application Architect and IT Project Manager.

Claire Stranack

Co-Founder & Design Lead

(BSc in Internet Design, University of Plymouth)

Claire is currently studying user interface design and programming at the University of Plymouth, so provides up to the minute guidance on current best practice for the User Experience.

Tim Stranack

Co-Founder & Business Lead

(MBA)

Tim has a history of success in founding and acting as the Business Development Lead for a series of successful technology businesses.
