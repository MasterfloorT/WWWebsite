Manager App

The Management Console is an internet portal to allow the management team to have an overview of all activity across their theatre (or theatre group):

  • Dashboard view of bar sales for each bar and overall daily & monthly figures
  • Access to all customer data to enable market analysis and future targeted marketing campaigns
  • Ability to add bars & staff and amend separate menus for each bar
  • Ability to analyse best selling items and customer spending patterns for each bar
  • Full configurability of bars, staff, menus, customer locations, seat or table service with the aim of a venue being completely self-sufficient in their use of the system
