Customer App

The Customer app is a smartphone app to enable theatre goers to order & pay for drinks, food or merchandise from their theatre seats or at a bar or café within the venue:




Customers can pre-order drinks and merchandise, so their order is available as they arrive, reducing the need for crowding and movement around the theatre




WirelessWaiter will provide a hub for Customers to interact with theatres and theatre companies they choose to follow




Initially targeted at drinks, food & merchandise purchasing, long-term looking at other innovative ways to increase theatre revenue through technology

Covid Safety

  • The Customer app collects customer information that can be used for ‘Track & Trace’
  • The Customer app reduces the need for unnecessary movement around the venue and aims to reduce crowding at bars and merchandise outlets
