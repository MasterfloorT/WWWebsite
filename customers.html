Customer App

The Customer app is a smartphone app to enable theatre goers to order & pay for drinks, food or merchandise from their theatre seats or at a bar or café within the venue:

  • A theatre has the ability to choose ‘seat service’ or ‘collection service’ – collection allows targeted seats to be directed to the nearest pick-up point, showing one-way routes and pick-up times to reduce congestion
  • Pre-ordering option so drinks & merchandise can be available as theatre goers arrive, again reducing the need for movement around the theatre
  • Initially targeted at drinks, food & merchandise purchasing, long-term looking at other innovative ways to increase theatre revenue through technology

Covid Safety

  • The app collects customer information that can be used for ‘Track & Trace’
  • The app reduces the need for unnecessary movement around the venue
