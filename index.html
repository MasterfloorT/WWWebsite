Wireless Waiter is a remote ordering platform allowing customers to order and pay for food, drinks or merchandise from their smartphones. Wireless Waiter has been designed with additional functionality to help theatres as they reopen after Covid19, but the platform also works for bars, cafes, nightclubs, museums and festivals, amongst others! We are passionate about helping the arts and hospitality industries get back to full health after Covid19.

Give your customers great service at a safe distance. Wireless Waiter enables your audience or bar customers to order their food, drinks and merchandise without even having to visit a counter. Safe service with more efficient staff. There are no upfront costs for a venue to start using Wireless Waiter, so start using today and open your doors to the public with a COVID secure environment.

The platform consists of three apps, please see more detail below.




❮ ❯



Customer App

A Customer app, designed for a smart phone – enables theatre goers to order and pay for food, drinks and merchandise from their phones







Bar App

A Bar app, designed for a tablet – enables each bar or outlet within a venue to view and fulfil all orders coming from the Customer app

❮ ❯


❮ ❯



Manager App

A Management console – an internet portal providing management and customer information and allowing a venue to actively manage their bars, staff and menus
