Safer Service

Give your customers great service at a safe distance. Wireless Waiter enables your audience to order their drinks, food and merchandise without even having to visit a counter. Safer service and more efficient staff allocation. Start using Wireless Waiter for free and open your doors to the public with a COVID secure environment.

Customer App

Our customer app incorporates sophisticated design and seamless functionality for the ease and satisfaction of the customer. We aim to keep the customer involved with every step of the process so they can have total transparency on the whereabouts of their order, while feeling safe in their seat.







Bar App

The bar app is created with efficiency and practicality at the forefront. With an easy to use interface that is perfectly in sync with the customer application. The app makes use of easy to follow ‘statuses’ for each order and overviews of all previous orders to keep organised during peak times.
